Calendar Interaction Guide:

Navigating Dates:

- Click on the month displayed at the top to navigate to a specific date.
- A prompt will appear for you to enter a date in the format yyyy-mm-dd.
- Utilize the "Previous" and "Next" buttons on the header to move between months.
- Use the "Today" button to quickly jump to the current date.

Adding Events:

- Click on any day in the calendar to add an event for that date.
- A prompt will appear for you to enter the event name with a maximum of 25 characters.

Removing Events:

- Click on an existing event to remove it.
- A prompt will appear to let you confirm or cancel the deletion.

Notes:

- If there are numerous events on a single date, you can scroll to view all.
- While the calendar is mobile-responsive, events on smaller screens may be visible but not readable. Consider using a larger screen for optimal readability.
- Certain holidays/events are included in the calendar and cannot be deleted.
- Your manually added events are locally stored on your browser. Ensure your browser settings allow local storage for a seamless experience.

Enjoy organizing your schedule with our interactive calendar!
