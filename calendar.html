Calendar Interaction Guide:

Navigating Dates:

- Click on the month displayed at the top to jump to a specific date.
- Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons on the header to move between months.
- Use the "Today" button to jump to the current date.

Adding Events:

- Click on any day in the calendar to add an event for that date.
- A prompt will appear for you to enter the event name.

Removing Events:

- Click on an existing event to remove it.
- A prompt will appear to let you confirm or cancel the deletion.

Notes:

- Events are visible but not readable on mobile devices. Please use a larger screen for optimal visibility.
- Some holidays/events are included to the calendar and cannot be deleted.
- Your manually added events are locally stored on your browser. Ensure your browser settings allow local storage for a seamless experience.

Enjoy organizing your schedule with our interactive calendar!
